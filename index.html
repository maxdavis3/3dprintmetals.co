Expert Material Selection

Choosing the right alloy is as important as the printing process — our material experts match performance requirements to the optimal certified metal.

  • ● AlSi10Mg: 40% lighter than equivalent steel
  • ● 17-4 PH: equals forged H13 tool steel performance
  • ● 316L: superior pitting corrosion resistance

  • ● Inconel 718: aerospace turbine qualification
  • ● CoCr: ISO 22674 dental prosthetic certification
